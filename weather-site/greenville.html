Firefighters help in Franklin Fires

A series of severe wildfires have erupted in the surroundings of the city of franklin this week, burning more than 1000 acres. High winds, unusually high temperatures and a historic drought have all contributed to one of the worst and earliest fire seasons in recent years. In response to this the Greenville and Springfiled Firefighters team up to help Franklin.
